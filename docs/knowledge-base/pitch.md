The short term opportunity
Many businesses are integrating AI. But five services with siloed mediocre AIs does not get you the leap that AI has promised. Fusing all your intelligence into a single prompt for an AI is simply not possible in any organization as of 2026. You will need to un-shard your data. And that means either living with the current generations of SaaS and mirroring your data into a specialized database, with all the risks that entails (including your SaaS vendor going out of business). Or taking the 5% of features you use across 20 different SaaS products and building your own internal platform, which self improves. 

To the moon.
There's a missed opportunity in using AI to copilot software development; we should be able to create orders of magnitude better software. Your corner store can have in house software at the standard of Nasa rockets. It means dropping some assumptions about how software development has worked over the years. Calypso is opinionated, but it moves to the tune of AI agents. Much of the development flow and tooling is for humans, and we can likely drop that.
